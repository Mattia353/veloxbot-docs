# Privacy Policy - VeloxBot

**Last Updated:** 28/01/2026

## 1. Introduction

VeloxBot ("the Bot") is a Discord bot designed for server partnership management with chain system functionality. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data
When you add VeloxBot to your Discord server, we collect:

- **Server ID** (Discord's unique identifier for your server)
- **Channel IDs** (for partnership channels and logging)
- **User IDs** (for administrators using bot commands)
- **Timestamps** (of partnership requests, approvals, and activities)
- **Server name and icon** (publicly available information)

### 2.2 Data We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ Email addresses
- ❌ Passwords or authentication tokens
- ❌ Private messages between users
- ❌ Voice data or recordings
- ❌ Payment information (bot is free)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Bot Functionality
Your data is used exclusively for:
- Processing partnership requests between servers
- Managing chain connections in the network system
- Sending notifications about partnership status
- Logging bot activities for moderation purposes
- Generating statistics about partnership networks

### 3.2 Data Processing
All data processing occurs through:
- **Discord's Official API** (following their terms)
- **Secure database** (encrypted at rest)
- **European servers** (GDPR compliant where applicable)

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

### 4.1 Storage Duration
We retain your data for:
- **Active servers:** As long as VeloxBot remains in your server
- **Inactive servers:** 30 days after bot removal
- **Backups:** 90 days maximum
